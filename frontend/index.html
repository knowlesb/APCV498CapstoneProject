Album Recognition

My collection

This app reads text from album cover images(OCR), searches Spotify for matching releases, and lets you save albums to a personal list backed by your account database.

How to use it

  • Images: JPG, PNG, or HEIC · max 5 MB per file.
  • OCR: Straight on shots should be taken with readable artist and title text; reflections and blurriness affect accuracy.
  • Flow: Take a picture or choose from your camera/upload → Click upload → Select the proper Spotify track → Save. All this can be viewed in My collection.
  • Live camera: It works in the browser as long as you give permission for the camera to be accessed; it can be done via HTTPS or localhost.
